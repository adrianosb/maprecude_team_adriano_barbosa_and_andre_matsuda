St. Augustine Under Three Flags: Tourist Guide and History

by W.J. Harris Company

Detailed descriptions of the various landmarks of St. Augustine from its founding in 1565 through 1918.

Source: W. J. Harris Company. (1918). St. Augustine Under Three Flags: Tourist Guide and History.

"St. Augustine: Founding of St. Augustine"
An account of the violence involved in the original settlements of the new world.
"St. Augustine: English Occupation"
The results of the English occupation on the city of St. Augustine.
"St. Augustine: Seminole War"
The major events and players of the Seminole War are described, including important treaties, battles, and chiefs.
"St. Augustine: Description of Fort Marion"
Detailed information about the construction and features of the building which became known as Fort Marion.
"St. Augustine: Guide to St. Augustine as it is Today"
Detailed descriptions of the various landmarks of St. Augustine as they were in 1918.
