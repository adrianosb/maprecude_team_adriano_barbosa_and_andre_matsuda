Andrew Lang's Fairy Books of Many Colors

Scottish writer, literary critic, historian, anthropologist, and scholar Andrew Lang published an immensely influential and popular series of books collecting folklore and fairy tales between 1889 and 1910. He collected stories from many sources, publishing the first English translations of several.

Books

The Blue Fairy Book (1889)

by Andrew Lang

This book is a collection of traditional tales. The collection was assembled by Scottish folklorist Andrew Lang although authorship of the stories is unknown. Lang published several collections of traditional tales, collectively known as Andrew Lang's Fairy Books.
