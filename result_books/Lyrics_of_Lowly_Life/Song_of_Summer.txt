Dis is gospel weathah sho’— Hills is sawt o’ hazy. Meddahs level ez a flo’ Callin’ to de lazy. Sky all white wif streaks o’ blue, Sunshine softly gleamin’, D’ain’t no wuk hit’s right to do, Nothin’ ’s right but dreamin’. Dreamin’ by de rivah side Wif de watahs glist’nin’, Feelin’ good an’ satisfied Ez you lay a–list’nin’ To the little nakid boys Splashin’ in de watah, Hollerin’ fu’ to spress deir joys Jes’ lak youngsters ought to. Squir’l a–tippin’ on his toes, So ’s to hide an’ view you; Whole flocks o’ camp–meetin’ crows Shoutin’ hallelujah. Peckahwood erpon de tree Tappin’ lak a hammah; Jaybird chattin’ wif a bee, Tryin’ to teach him grammah. Breeze is blowin’ wif perfume, Jes’ enough to tease you; Hollyhocks is all in bloom, Smellin’ fu’ to please you. Go ‘way, folks, an’ let me ‘lone, Times is gettin’ dearah— Summah’s settin’ on de th’one, An’ I ‘m a–layin’ neah huh!
